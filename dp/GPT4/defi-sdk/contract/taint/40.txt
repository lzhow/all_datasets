Reason: The variable `callData` in the function `approveAndCall` is passed as an argument to the function `callBytes`, which is an external function and can be called by anyone. The `callerCallData` received by `callBytes` is decoded to extract `callData`, among other parameters. Since `callerCallData` is provided by the caller of the `callBytes` function, and `callBytes` is external and does not restrict who can call it, the `callData` variable is indeed controlled by the user.
