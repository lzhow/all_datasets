The ` lock` function is used to prevent reentrancy attacks by temporarily disabling the ability to call the contract's functions. This is done by setting the local `locked ` variable to`0`. The function then executes the desired logic, and at its end, sets the` locked`variable back`1`. This ensures that the functions cannot be called again until the next time the  lock is released.
