
The variable addedvalue in increaseallowance() is a control variable that is used to increase the allowable amount of tokens that can be spent by a particular address. It is important to note that this function is called by an external account (such as a contract or user wallet) and is therefore not considered to be a user-controlled variable. Therefore, the answer to your question is no, added value is considered not to have user control.
