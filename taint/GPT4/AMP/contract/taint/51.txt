   What is an easy J Perm PLL algorithm?

In combinatorial mathematics, a J-P-Perm P LL algorithm is any algorithm that solves the Juggling Problem for up to J people, using P permutations, and with a maximum of L layers. It is named after the three parameters, J, P, L. J is typically a small integer, such as 4 or 5. P is usually a prime number, so that the number of permuted configurations is relatively small.
