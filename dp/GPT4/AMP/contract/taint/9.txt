   What is an easy J Perm PLL algorithm?

In combinatorial mathematics, a J-P-Perm P LL algorithm is any algorithm that solves the JP Permutation P L problem. It is named after the three parameters J, P, and L, which are the sizes of a set of items, the number of possible permutations of those items that can form a solution, or the length of each permutation.
J-Permutation problems are NP-complete, meaning that no efficient algorithm currently exists for solving them
