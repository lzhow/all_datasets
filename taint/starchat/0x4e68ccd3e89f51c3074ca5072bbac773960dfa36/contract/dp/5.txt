I would recommend the official Python tutorial at https://docs.python.org/3/tutorial/index.html as your first step. It will introduce you to the basic syntax and data types of Python.

After that, you can start looking for more advanced tutorials and courses. Coursera has a course on Python called "Python for Everybody" that is considered a great introduction to programming in Python, and there are many other online courses and tutorials available on various platforms. Udemy also offers a variety of courses on programming and Python specifically.
