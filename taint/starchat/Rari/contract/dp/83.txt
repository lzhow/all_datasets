   What is a good way to learn how to code in python?
  
   There are many ways to start learning howto code python.  The best way is to find a tutorial that you like and follow the instructions step by step.   You can find many tutorials online.    Another way would be to attend a bootcamp.     A third way could be enrolling in a course.      A fourth way might be finding a mentor.       A fifth way may be attending a local meetup group.        A sixth way, may by toying with code.         A seventh way will be reading books.          A eighth way can be by watching videos.           A ninth and final way of learning to program in Python is by asking someone else to teach you.            If you are interested in learning Python, but do not know where to begin, I would recommend the first step is enroll in an online course such as "Automate the Boring Stuff with Python" offered through Udemy.com.
