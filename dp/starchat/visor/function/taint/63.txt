
The variable _allowance is a mapping in Solidty that stores the allowance of an owner address over a spender address. It is used to track how much of a token an address is allowed to spend from another address, and is set by calling the approve function.

In this contract, the _approve function is called by an account (the owner) to set the amount of tokens that the sponsor (msg sender) is authorized to transfer from the owner's account. This mapping is therefore controlled directly by users of the contract.
