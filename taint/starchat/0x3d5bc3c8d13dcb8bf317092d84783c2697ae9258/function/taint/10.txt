  An incremental bundling tool is designed to optimize the performance of web application development by bundl ingtogether all the assets (JavaScript, CSS, images, etc.) required by a web page into a single file. This reduces the number of requests made to the server, which improves the page load time and reduces latency.

Webpack is widely used as an incremental bundle tool due to its flexibility and ease of use. It can be integrated with a variety of development tools
